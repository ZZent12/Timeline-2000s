Patroit Act is passed | Politics

45 days after 9/11 attacks congress passed the Patriot Act. This was a bill saying it was used to stop terrorist attacks by increasing surveillance on the population. After being deemed unconstitual many times the act was again on trial agianst an eariler decree. Mainly a part call section 220 which allowed the new social media companies that were growing at the time to not be liable for what their users say. After the wide spread adoption of end to end encryption (E2EE) in 2016 allowing not even the owning company to look at the data sent by its users The Patriot Act was called into question again because it basically became useless and in a court case it was ruled that E2EE can not be forced to be broken by the government, although it did not stop them trying again 4 years later in 2020.
